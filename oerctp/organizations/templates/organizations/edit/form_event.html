Complete this form to create or edit information about an OER event at your institution. Events are one-time actions such as workshops, talks, conferences, or presentations about OER that were organized at or by your institution. Events generally do not include off-campus events you have attended. You may include future events if you wish, although the primary purpose is to track past events.

When you click submit, you will receive a link where you may return to edit this form in the future. To create additional entries for activities at your institution, return to your institutional homepage or contact your local Point of Contact.

How this information will be used: The purpose of this project is to create a public directory about institutional activities on OER. As such, the information you enter will be shared publicly through the directory (except for email addresses and where otherwise marked), and released under a CC0 Public Domain Dedication to maximize the reusability of the project’s dataset. You will be asked to confirm these terms before submitting this form. Please ensure you fully understand how your information will be used and shared by reviewing our FAQ before proceeding.
