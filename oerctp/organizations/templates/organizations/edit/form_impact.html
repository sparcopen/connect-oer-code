Complete this form to create or edit an Annual Impact Report for your institution. These reports collect basic metrics about OER use and progress on campus to track the impact of OER over time. These reports are intended to be updated frequently as you gather more information about your campus, both during the current year and for past years.

We encourage you to review the Annual Impact Report FAQ before filling this out, as it addresses many common questions.

Please note that reports are done per academic year, meaning that this report should only include information about OER activities during the academic year selected. You can create reports for additional years once you return to your institution's home page, and you can return to update this form at any time.
