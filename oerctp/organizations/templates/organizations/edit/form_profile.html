Please use the form below to complete your Connect OER Institutional Profile. This profile provides basic information about OER at your institution, including a designated contact person (who may be private or public), a narrative overview of OER activities, and answers to a set of standard multiple choice questions. The first time you fill this out it may take 20-25 minutes, but in the future you will only need to update information that has changed.

How this information will be used: The purpose of this project is to create a public directory about institutional activities on OER. As such, the information you enter will be shared publicly through the directory (except for email addresses and where otherwise marked), and released under a CC0 Public Domain Dedication to maximize the reusability of the project's dataset. You will be asked to confirm these terms before submitting this form. Please ensure you fully understand how your information will be used and shared by reviewing our FAQ before proceeding.

Thank you for taking the time to complete this form. It will ensure your institution is represented in the Connect OER directory, and that others can learn more about your work.

Point of Contact

Each institution is required to designate a Point of Contact for the purposes of this project. The Point of Contact will be sent all relevant information about the project, including reminders to update data and questions submitted about your directory page via a secure contact form. In most cases, the Point of Contact will be a member of Library staff, and you may update this information at any time. It is recommended for Points of Contact to be listed in the directory by name, although you will have the option to hide the name if desired. If you wish to provide additional contact instructions for directory readers, you may do so in your free-form institutional abstract.
